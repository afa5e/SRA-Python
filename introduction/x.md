# X

This task will require you to propt the user for an odd integer, n, that is greater than 5. Your program should then print out a n*n pattern of asterisks in the shape of a X.

## Example:

```
Enter size: 5
*   *
 * * 
  *  
 * * 
*   *
```

```
Enter size: 9
*       *
 *     * 
  *   *  
   * *   
    *    
   * *   
  *   *  
 *     * 
*       *
```
